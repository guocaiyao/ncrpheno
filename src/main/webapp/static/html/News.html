    • News


  • Sep 20, 2019, The ncRPheno version 2.0 was released.

    • The ncRPheno platform version 2.0 contains 482,751 non-redundant associations between 14494 ncRNAs and 3210 disease phenotypes across 11 species from 25642 publications. The numbers of associations included in the ncRPheno version 2.0 are approximately 4.64-fold of those in the ncRPheno version 1.0. Moreover, compared with the version 1.0, the version 2.0 offers extra applications for further analysis and visualization on the association data, including ncRNA prioritization, network visualization, disease-tree visualization and word-cloud visualization.

  • December 27, 2018, Oral presentation in the ICCCB 2018 Symposium

    • The ncRPheno platform was presented in the 2018 2nd International Conference on Computational Chemistry and Biology (ICCCB 2018) in the keynote speaker session, Hong Kong, China.


  • October 23, 2018, Oral presentation in the CCSBS Symposium.

    • The ncRPheno platform was presented in the 8th National Conference on Bioinformatics and Systems Biology of China and the 1st (Macao) International Bioinformatics Symposium in the oral presentation session, Macao, China.


  • Jun 25, 2018, The ncRPheno version 1.0 was released.

    • The ncRPheno platform version 1.0 contains 103,932 non-redundant associations between 12,447 ncRNAs and 1,998 disease phenotypes with supporting evidence in publications. ncRPheno provides search, browse, RESTful Web service APIs and data download applications.


  • Apr 10, 2018, Poster presentation in Biocuration 2018.

    • The ncRPheno platform was poster presented in the 2018 11th International Biocuration Conference, Shanghai, China.
